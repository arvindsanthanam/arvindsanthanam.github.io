D3 Visualizations

Data-Driven Documents(D3) is a JavaScript library used for visualizing data using web standards. By using D3.js, visualizations were createdbased on different topics to help provide insight into each domain. The created webpagesalso make use HTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript, AngularJS and Angular Material libraries to help support the webpage.


Auto-Immune Hepatitis Visualization


Autoimmune Hepatitis (AIH) is a rare and incurable non-viral disease which affects the liver. Due to its rarity, support for patients with this disease is often provided over social media platforms to patients who ask health and medical questions about their disease and how it affects them.

Our visualization depicts the links 280 people who actively discuss the subject, to a set of 50 most frequently discussed topics to analyze trends and patterns which can help user gain insights about the disease.

Energy Visualization of Data from the US Energy Information Administration


With data from the US Energy Information Administration, the webpage is designed and developed to view/ compare production and consumption values of different resources for over 230 regions. The data is of Total Primary Energy, Total Electricity, Renewable Electricity, Renewable Biofuels, Petroleum, Coal and Energy-related CO2 emissions.
