Context

It is common for amateur and learning musicians to find difficulty in following their musical interest.One reason worth emphasizing is the lack of a musical clique for people to learn, collaborate and socialize with. We aim at designing an application for amateur musicians to connect, meet other musicians within their community, and pursue their musical passion side-by-side with their other responsibilities in life.


Design Solution

"Lorem Ipsum has been the industry's standard dummy "

Through a series of interviews and questionnaires, the optimal solution indicated a smartphone applicationdesign for people to be able to view groups and user profiles of other musicians registered within the same vicinity, and socialize and collaborate/jam with people they prefer, based on their profile description, musical knowledge and the relative distance, based on in-built filters.

Domain exploration and Data collection:
The design process began with surveys and interviews which explored peoples background, musical & collaborative experience,practice habits and their views on an application to help them connect with other musicians.


Full Survey Form   

Concept Modeling:
The thoughts and opinions that were gathered were appropriately sorted based on similarity through affinity mapping for a better understanding of people’s opinions toward the solution and the features they would expect in the application.

Ideation:
With the process of persona development and brainstorming, user scenarios were observed andfurther analysis, including sketching and storyboarding, was used to define scalability and limitations of the application.


Prototype Design:
After designing the lo-fi prototype, an internal heuristic and cognitive walkthrough was conducting and corresponding issues were fixed in a hi-fidelity prototype to have it tested by users over scenarios using the think-aloud protocol to indicate final changes to the design.


User testing revealed that few considerations to be taken for people to start using the application. Chief among them was safety. Since people canbe expected to meet in person, it is important for people to attempt meeting at public venues, and travel elsewhere at their own caution. Additionally, a background check is to be conducted before people are registered to use the service for safety reasons. These features will be incorporated in future designs.
