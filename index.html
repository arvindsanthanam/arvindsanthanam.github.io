About me

"The greatest project you'll ever work on is you."

Currently pursuing my master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction(HCI), I envision myself working on the design and usability of innovative technologies. With experience in web-design and development, my goal is to expand my knowledge to create designs that improve intuitiveness and efficiency over ubiquitous interfaces.

Through my dual education, I have collected skills both as a designer as well as a developer to allow myself to model, code, as well as understand the architecture behind applications. As my passion endears design, I have my aptitude focused on Contextual Design Methods ranging from formative, to generative, to summative techniques that help me deliver the best solutions.

I see myself more enthusiastic during generative phases of design as I see a confluence of ideas from a variety of thought that border or propel a solution. Working under such conditions make it feel like a 'puzzle of people' that I very much enjoy working for.

Beyond work, I am fascinated by subjects such as behavioral science over human motivation, social psychology, and lifestyle habits which help me perpetually seek improvement as an individual. When I am not working, I also find myself swimming, cooking, or learning guitar to keep myself happy.

Following my graduation in May 2018, I will be willing to relocate to pursue the profession that I love.
