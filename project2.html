Context

Mu Sigma is business solutions provider who is often approached by retail firmsas a consultant to provide analytics overa supplier’s demand for price hikes over their products.Price hikes happen for a variety of reasons; due to anincrease in the cost of raw materials,cost of transportation, or due to bad weather.

A singular web application for the collectivemanagement and communication of all CPI (cost price increase) requests from the supplier,with analytics for each request provided by Mu Sigma, was required to be designed for improved communication and to overcome certain gaps that were existing in Mu Sigma’s delivery system. The system was also required to achieveabstraction over the involvement of Mu-Sigmabetween the client and the suppliers.

The Process

Requirement analysis:
Client meetings focusing on product expectations, the environment it would be deployed in and the tools that had/could be used to build the system. The requirement gathering resulted in a document with a list of functional, and non-functional requirements (scalability, stability, performance, and compatibility) for the product.


Concept Modeling:
Use-Case diagrams, database class diagrams and sequence diagrams were designed following literature review over the toolsthat would be used to implement the design. Through brainstorming, various solutions were discussed and corresponding interface designs were sketched to analyze pros and cons of each solution.


Prototype Design:
Initial prototype with company colors and the application’s functionality was presented to the client for feedback. The client provided substantial recommendationregarding the functionality of the design, and expected a more accurate depiction of the interface for their analysis.


‹ ›

A second version of the prototype incorporating the clients feedback was developed and showcased. The prototype used design elementsfrom the technology planned to use; to make the prototype look more like the final product. On showcasing, few aesthetic aspects were asked for improvement and a third version of the prototype was created.


Development and testing:
The project is built on top of a MVC architecture. This architectural pattern helps enable development by splitting the application into three mainlogical components and by theseparation of components, handling specific developmental aspects of the application became easier. The application used python Django framework and used web development tools: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, AngularJS, JQuery, Bootstrap to support the application. The application went through unit testing using Karma-runner and Angular Batarang for debugging.

The system is currently in use at Mu Sigma and the functionality for the system to recommend a price reduction has been designated for future work
